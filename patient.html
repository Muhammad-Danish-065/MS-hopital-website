Patient Information

At Muhammad Shah Poly Clinic, we prioritize providing comprehensive and accessible information to our patients. Our Patient Information page is designed to help you navigate your healthcare experience with ease. Here, you can find essential details about scheduling appointments, understanding billing procedures, and accessing necessary patient forms. We aim to ensure that every aspect of your visit is seamless and transparent, allowing you to focus on your health and well-being. Our dedicated team is always available to assist you with any questions or concerns, ensuring that you receive the highest quality care and support throughout your journey with us.

Appointments

At Muhammad Shah Poly Clinic, scheduling an appointment with our expert doctors is simple and convenient. We offer flexible timings to accommodate your busy schedule. Our online appointment system allows you to book a slot that suits you best. For urgent care, walk-ins are also welcome. Our goal is to provide timely and efficient care, ensuring that you receive the medical attention you need without unnecessary delays

Billing

We strive to make the billing process as straightforward and transparent as possible. Our billing department is available to answer any questions you might have regarding your charges and payment options. We accept a variety of insurance plans and offer flexible payment solutions to ensure that financial concerns do not become a barrier to receiving quality healthcare. For your convenience, online bill payment options are also available.

Patient Forms

To streamline your visit, we offer downloadable patient forms that you can complete prior to your appointment. These forms include medical history, consent forms, and insurance information. By filling out these forms in advance, you can save time during your visit and help our staff provide you with the most efficient care. All patient information is kept confidential and secure in compliance with privacy regulations.
